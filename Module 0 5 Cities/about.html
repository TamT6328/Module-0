About us

We are Africa's largest travel guidance platform.

Today, South Africa stands proud, promising visitors a spectacular destination that will stay close to their hearts long after their departure.

TravelNow, the Africa's largest travel guidance platform, helps hundreds of millions of people each month become better travelers, from planning to booking to taking a trip.
Travelers across the globe use the TravelNow site and app to discover where to stay, what to do and where to eat based on guidance from those who have been there before.
