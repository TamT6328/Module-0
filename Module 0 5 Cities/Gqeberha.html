THE CITY OF GQEBERHA, EASTERN CAPE

The Nelson Mandela Metro (including Gqeberha, Uitenhage and Despatch) is the gateway to the Eastern Cape Region, its well-equipped airport and harbour linking South Africa with other national and international destinations. Known as the "Friendly City", Gqeberha is located on the south-eastern coast, 763 km east of Cape Town.
A superb value-for-money holiday base, Gqeberha offers a diverse selection of attractions as a family-fun holiday destination including scenic nature trails, historic heritage, magnificent wildlife, cultural experiences and countless water sport activities.
